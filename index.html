BECHTEL IN INDIA

Bechtel India is serving as a major Project Execution office for Bechtel’s Global Business Units providing Basic and Front-End Design, Engineering, Procurement, Construction, IT and Support services to projects in Infrastructure, Energy, Mining & Metal and Manufacturing & Technology markets around the globe. Bechtel India offers a full suite of services supporting all phases of the project from concept studies, including feasibility, FEED, and pre-FEED studies to full EPC/ EPCM project execution.
Bechtel in India operates from Gurgaon office near the Indian Capital and from Vadodara office in Western India. Both offices are equipped with the most modern automation, infrastructure, and core communication systems. The composite team has delivered large and medium sized projects to our customers across six continents in the world.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Alongside delivering excellence to our customers, Bechtel India is committed to driving positive impact on the local communities. Our colleagues work with the communities to improve the economic, educational, and social environment with an emphasis on promoting education among women and specially abled persons.
Donation of Ambulance to Indian Red Cross Society
Bechtel India recently provided the Red Cross Society of Haryana State with an ambulance in keeping with Bechtel’s commitment towards promotion of healthcare in the local community. The ambulance is equipped with latest technology essential for emergency treatment of patients until they arrive in hospital.
Building Biogas plant in village of Chipiya, Madhya Pradesh
Bechtel volunteers partnered with Engineers Without Borders USA, Reliance Foundation, and the village of Chipiya in India to build a biogas plant. The new facility transforms cow manure into a clean, sustainable energy source.
EWB in Sirohi India
Bechtel volunteers worked to provide much needed solar-powered LED lighting to the rural Indian village of Sirohi. The Engineers Without Borders lighting project, aided by Bechtel funding in collaboration with other nonprofit partners, was the first major effort of Bechtel in New Delhi.
